Storm Shadow Missiles: Ukraine's Pivotal Armament in the Ongoing Conflict

國際新聞

The Storm Shadow, an Anglo-French cruise missile with a formidable range of approximately 250km, has emerged as a pivotal armament in Ukraine's arsenal against Russian aggression. This sophisticated weapon, known as Scalp in France, is deployed from aircraft and navigates at near-sonic speeds, hugging the terrain before decimating its intended target.

These missiles are lauded for their precision in penetrating fortified structures and ammunition depots. Despite their exorbitant cost of nearly US$1 million per unit, they have proven instrumental in striking critical targets, such as Russia's Black Sea naval headquarters, effectively neutralizing the Russian navy's dominance in Crimea.

Ukraine has been fervently advocating for the abolition of restrictions on utilizing these missiles against targets within Russian territory. The reticence of Western allies stems from apprehensions about potential escalation, particularly the risk of Russia retaliating against NATO countries, which could precipitate a wider conflict.

If restrictions are rescinded, it could pose a significant strategic quandary for Russia in positioning its air defenses. However, some military analysts posit that while beneficial, Storm Shadow missiles are unlikely to alter the trajectory of the war substantially. This is due to limited supplies and Russia's preemptive measures of relocating key assets deeper into its hinterland, beyond the missiles' operational range.

風暴之影導彈：烏克蘭在持續衝突中的關鍵武器

風暴之影導彈,一種英法聯合研製的巡航導彈,擁有約250公里的強大射程,已成為烏克蘭對抗俄羅斯侵略的武器庫中的關鍵武器。這種先進武器在法國被稱為"斯卡爾普",它從飛機上發射,以接近音速的速度飛行,貼地掠過地形,最終精確摧毀預定目標。

這些導彈因其精準打擊加固建築和彈藥庫的能力而備受讚揚。儘管每枚導彈的造價高達近100萬美元,但它們在打擊關鍵目標方面已證明極為有效,比如成功襲擊了俄羅斯黑海艦隊總部,有效削弱了俄羅斯海軍在克里米亞的優勢地位。

烏克蘭一直在熱烈倡導取消使用這些導彈攻擊俄羅斯境內目標的限制。西方盟國的猶豫源於對潛在衝突升級的擔憂,特別是俄羅斯可能對北約國家進行報復,這可能引發更大規模的衝突。

如果這些限制被取消,俄羅斯在部署防空系統時將面臨重大戰略困境。然而,一些軍事分析家認為,儘管風暴之影導彈有益,但不太可能實質性地改變戰爭走向。這是因為導彈供應有限,而且俄羅斯已採取預防措施,將關鍵資產轉移到更深入的腹地,超出了這些導彈的作戰範圍。
